### DAFNI platform

DAFNI platform will be used to support coding. For these accounts will be provided.

Once you get your account, please login in in this link: [https://facility.secure.dafni.rl.ac.uk/](https://facility.secure.dafni.rl.ac.uk/) and enter your login and password.
