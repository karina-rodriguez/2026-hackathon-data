This resource aims to prepare Arts and Humanities (A&H) researchers for supporting the [Hackathon: Data Driven Approaches to Safeguarding Cultural Heritage](https://www.diskah.org/event-details/hackathon-data-driven-approaches-to-safeguarding-cultural-heritage). 

The content of the resource includes information, links and other shared resources. It is a resource to support those ideas, tools and networks developed at the event. 
The event is organized by the [DISKAH](https://www.diskah.org/) Network, the [Archaeology Data Service](https://archaeologydataservice.ac.uk/), the [Data/Culture](https://datacultureportal.uk/) AHRC-funded project and [DAFNI](https://www.sc.stfc.ac.uk/programmes/dafni/).
